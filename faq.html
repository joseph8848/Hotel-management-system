Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your stay.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in at Champion Hotel begins at 3:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. If you require a late check-out or early check-in, please contact our concierge through your customer dashboard.

Is early check-in or late check-out available?

Early check-in and late check-out are subject to room availability and may incur an additional fee. VIP and Diamond members enjoy complimentary late check-out privileges.

Do you offer airport shuttle services?

Yes, we provide luxury airport transfers from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) and Wilson Airport. You can arrange this via the Support tab in your Customer Dashboard after booking.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

This depends on the package you select during booking. Our Executive and Luxury Suites include a complimentary signature breakfast buffet.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our acclaimed restaurant caters to various dietary needs including vegan, gluten-free, and halal. Please mention your requirements in the 'Special Requests' box when booking.

Can I cancel my reservation without a penalty?

Cancellations made 48 hours prior to your check-in date will not incur any penalties. Late cancellations or no-shows are subject to a fee equal to the first night's room rate.

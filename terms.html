Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Please read these terms carefully before using our services.

1. Booking and Cancellation Policy

All reservations must be guaranteed with a valid credit card. Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in will be subject to a fee equivalent to one night's room charge plus applicable taxes.

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. A valid government-issued ID and the credit card used for booking must be presented at the front desk upon arrival.

Champion Hotel respects your privacy. We collect personal information solely for the purpose of managing reservations, accommodating special requests, and complying with local hospitality laws. Your data is never sold to third parties.
